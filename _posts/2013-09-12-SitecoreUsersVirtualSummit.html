The great guys at Hedgehog Development (oh which I am now one) are holding the Sitecore Users Virtual Summit on the 15th October! Join me as I look at some of the more advanced features of Glass.Mapper.Sc.

Join the Glass.Mapper.Sc session to learn about some of the ways you can extend Glass.Mapper and some of the features you may not have used.

Currently I am thinking of covering the following topics but if you have anything specific you want to see let me know and I will see if I can fit it in.

  • A look at the different pipelines in Glass.Mapper and how they can be used to make development easier.
  • The Glass.Mapper.Sc.Razor project, why and how you can use it.
  • Using Glass.Mapper to make your code testable.
  • Integration with third parties

To sign up to the session go to the Glass.Mapper.Sc event page on the Sitecore Users Virtual Summit and also sign up to the other great sessions being run.
